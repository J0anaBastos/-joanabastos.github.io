About

I'm a Product Designer with 7+ years of experience designing complex digital products where clarity, trust, and accuracy are critical.

My background started in communication design, which shaped the way I think about visual systems, structure, and information clarity. Over the years, I transitioned into software and have worked across fintech, AI, and enterprise platforms, often in environments that are complex, data-heavy, and operationally critical.

I enjoy breaking down complexity, helping users navigate workflows that need to perform reliably in real-world conditions. My work focuses on simplifying systems, improving usability, and designing scalable products that support both user and business needs.

I work closely with product and engineering teams throughout the product lifecycle, contributing beyond interface design to help define problems, shape product direction, and deliver solutions that are practical, scalable, and impactful.
