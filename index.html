About

I'm a Product Designer with 7+ years of experience working on complex, high-stakes products where clarity and trust really matter.

I started in communication design, which shaped how I think about visual systems and clarity. Over time, I moved into software and have been working across fintech, AI, and enterprise tools, often in environments where things are complex, data-heavy, and mistakes have real consequences.

My work usually sits in that space where things can easily get overwhelming. I enjoy breaking that complexity down, structuring information in a way that makes sense, and designing flows that actually work in real-world conditions, not just ideal ones.

I tend to work closely with product and engineering, contributing beyond just the interface, helping define problems, shape solutions, and ship things that are useful, scalable, and make a measurable difference.
