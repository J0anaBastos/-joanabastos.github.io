Enterprise UX· Digitisation· Multi-Role Systems

Iberdrola Contract Platform

Introduction

Iberdrola's Portuguese operations ran contract management entirely on paper (pen-and-paper forms, printed client ID documents, etc). New employees took too long to become independent. Errors and missing data were routine.

Measured Outcomes
  • 50% reduction in new employee onboarding and platform adoption time.
  • Data entry errors and missing records significantly reduced.
  • Paper-based process fully eliminated enabling remote access and a searchable contract history for the first time.
The Design Challenge
  • Three distinct user roles with fundamentally different needs: contract managers needing oversight, field sales needing speed, technicians needing specific client and service data.
  • Replace a physical workflow, including printed ID document collection, with a structured digital system that feels faster, not more complex.
  • Surface expiring and at-risk contracts immediately on login without requiring users to search or filter manually.
  • Design modular contract data architecture so users could add, edit, or remove components independently without breaking the overall record structure.
What I Designed
  • Role-specific dashboard views with priority-surfaced contract status — expiring contracts and flagged cases visible on login without manual triage.
  • Modular contract architecture enabling users to interact with only the sections relevant to their role, reducing cognitive load for field staff.
  • Digital document collection flow replacing physical ID filing, enabling remote access, audit trails, and searchable records for the first time.
  • Unified status and management layer giving managers full contract oversight alongside responsible sales manager visibility in a single view.

⊞
Data Clusters
Modular systems designed to be easy to add, edit, or remove. Only the needed parts of a contract are shown at any time.
Each cluster is self-contained, reducing cognitive load and allowing different user roles to interact with only what's relevant to them.
◈
Status & Management
A single scan of the contract surface reveals all active statuses (expiring, pending, active, flagged) alongside the responsible sales manager. No searching, no filtering. Priority information is always visible on load.
☎
Call Recordings & Documents
All calls and documents exchanged between Iberdrola and the client are stored and surfaced directly on the contract record giving managers, sales staff, and technicians a full communication trail without switching tools or systems.
☰
Observations & History
Managers, field sales staff, and technicians can each add contextual notes directly to the contract and review the full chronological history of every step the contract has been through.
A shared source of truth across all roles, replacing scattered emails and verbal handoffs.
